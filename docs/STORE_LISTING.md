## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
Receipt Scanner is a fast, private expense tracker that turns paper receipts into structured spending data — entirely on your device.

KEY FEATURES

• Smart receipt scanning
Snap a photo or import from your gallery. On-device OCR extracts the store name, total amount, and date — no internet connection or login required.

• Automatic categorization
Spending is auto-classified across 10 categories: Groceries, Electronics, Dining, Pharmacy, Gas, Clothing, Entertainment, Travel, Healthcare, and Other.

• Manual entry
No receipt? Tap "Enter manually" to type details directly — perfect for cash purchases or quick notes.

• Monthly dashboard
See your total spending, top category, and per-category breakdown for any month at a glance, with a clean dark-mode chart view.

• Search & edit
Full-text search across stores, categories, and notes. Edit any field after saving — store name, amount, date, or category.

• 100% private, 100% offline
All receipts stay on your device. No accounts. No cloud sync. No analytics. No ads. The app never makes a network request.

PERMISSIONS

• Camera — only used when you tap "Camera" to photograph a receipt
• Photos — only used when you tap "Gallery" to import an existing image

WHY ON-DEVICE OCR?

Receipt Scanner uses Google ML Kit's on-device text recognition. Your receipts never leave your phone — there's no server to leak data, no API quota, and no internet required. Works on flights, in tunnels, anywhere.

PERFECT FOR

• Tracking grocery and dining spending across the month
• Categorizing expenses for tax season or reimbursements
• Capturing receipts before they fade or get lost
• Anyone who values privacy in their financial apps

Built with React Native + Expo + Google ML Kit. Open source on GitHub.
```

## Required screenshots

You need **2-8 phone screenshots**. Easy way:
1. Install the latest APK on your Android device
2. Open the app and screenshot:
   - Dashboard with at least 2-3 receipts saved
   - The scan screen (idle state with Camera/Gallery/Manual buttons)
   - The review/edit form
   - The history screen
   - The edit modal
3. Transfer the PNGs to your computer and upload to Play Console

Recommended sizes: **1080×2340** (or whatever your phone produces).
