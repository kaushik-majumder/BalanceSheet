# Hosting the privacy policy (GitHub Pages — free, 2 minutes)

The Play Store requires a public URL for your privacy policy. GitHub Pages is the simplest option.

## Steps

1. Go to **https://github.com/kaushik-majumder/BalanceSheet/settings/pages**
2. Under **Source**, select **Deploy from a branch**
3. Branch: **`main`**, folder: **`/docs`**
4. Click **Save**
5. Wait ~30 seconds for the first deploy

Your privacy policy will then be live at:

```
https://kaushik-majumder.github.io/BalanceSheet/privacy-policy.html
```

That's the URL you paste into the Play Console under **Store presence → Main store listing → Privacy policy**.

## Verify it's live

Open the URL in any browser. You should see the styled privacy policy. If you get a 404, GitHub Pages is still deploying — wait another minute and refresh.
