Receipt Scanner — Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-05-09

Receipt Scanner ("the app") is a personal expense-tracking utility that runs entirely on your device. This policy describes what the app does and does not do with your data.

What data the app accesses

  • Camera: only when you tap "Scan a Receipt" → "Camera". Used to capture an image of a paper receipt.
  • Photo library: only when you tap "Scan a Receipt" → "Gallery". Used to import an existing receipt image.
  • Receipt data you enter or capture: store name, amount, date, category, optional notes, and the receipt image you choose to keep.

Where the data is stored

All data — including the OCR-extracted text, the parsed fields, and any receipt images you save — is stored only on your device, in a local SQLite database (receipts.db) inside the app's private storage area.

What the app does not do

  • The app does not upload your receipts, images, or any other personal data to any server.
  • The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • The app does not share your data with anyone.
  • The app does not require an account, login, or internet connection to function.

How OCR (text recognition) works

Text extraction uses Google ML Kit (on Android) and Apple Vision (on iOS). Both run entirely on-device; receipt images are never transmitted to Google, Apple, or any other party.

Deleting your data

You can delete any individual receipt from inside the app (Edit → Delete). To remove all data, uninstall the app — Android removes the app's private storage on uninstall.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it does not transmit data off-device.

Changes to this policy

If material changes are made to how the app handles data, an updated version of this policy will be published at the same URL with a new effective date.
